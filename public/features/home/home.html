Overview

Force Reader is an experiment in data visualization and user interfaces. The data visualizations I have seen on the web typically restrict interactivity to mouseover popups and labels. I want to push the boundaries of what a data visualization can do and explore the possibilities of combining visualization and interactivity.

Technical Details

Force Reader is built with NodeJS, AngularJS and D3js.
